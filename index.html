I'm Mieka

I’m a new knitwear designer originally from NYC now living in Amsterdam. I seek to create designs that have a simple, textured look while incorporating elements that help enhance the function of the garment. I’m still discovering my style and I hope you enjoy my knitting journey as much as I do.

Thanks for following along! Feel free to shoot me a message just to say hi and if you’re in Amsterdam, let’s grab a coffee and knit a while!
